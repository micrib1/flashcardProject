How to Play:

The goal of this game is to correctly answer the question or definition posed by each flashcard 7 times without answering incorrectly. The game will cycle through the entire deck of flashcards each round. If you answer correctly, mark the flashcard correct, which will promote it to the next "level" of mastery. If you answer incorrectly, mark the flashcard incorrect, which will demote the card back to "Start." When a card reaches "Finish," it is considered mastered and it will not be cycled through anymore. Once all cards reach "Finish," the game is over and you have won!
  1. Select a deck from the menu
  2. Read the question on the card and try to think of the answer
  3. Press "Flip Card" to reveal the answer
  4. Press "Mark Correct" or "Mark Incorrect" depending on the accuracy of your answer
  5. Correct responses promote the flashcard to the next level, incorrect responses demote it to "Start"
  6. When all the cards reach "Finish," you have won the game!
